About Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans is a real-time Epic Combat strategy game that combines many complex elements, such as building, defending, and joining clans and going to War with other clans. Each detail makes an immense contribution to this game and gradually creates new game play, giving players a lot of stimulation even if they become late-game players.

Build your Village

The development of clash of clan and it's game play structure is unique and fresh and pioneer for other strategy trends. In this game, players wll have to build a mighty village, arrange it as their imagination, train soldiers, and conquer countless other people's villages. Not stopping there, they can enjoy countless special activities, and large-scale events for clans. Although everything happens in real-time, players still have to thoroughly research factors, including maintaining the village, upgrades and protecting resources.

Join a Clan

All players will become a king of their own village, thereby developing, expanding, and upgrading everything for maximum performance. Fortunately, the game will have many thorough instructions in constructing facilities or buildings, ensuring the village is stable, and creating many resources. Most of the time, the village will automatically create various items or other resources while the player is away as a bonus which makes clash of clans only more enjoyable.
