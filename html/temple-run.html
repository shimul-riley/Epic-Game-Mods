Behind the Story

Temple Run has a story behind. It tells the story of a group of explorers entering an ancient forest and stealing a statue of a god in a cursed temple. When they stepped outside, they realized a giant monkey was chasing them to reclaim something they shouldn't have. After all, there was only one survivor in the team of adventurers. He is holding the statue and trying to escape when death is chasing behind.
